About Me

Hi! I'm Luca, a Research Scholar at the University of Oxford in the Future of Humanity Institute. I have a background in economics, with additional competencies in data science and public policy. My research interests are centered around the long-term challenges facing humanity and their technological solutions.

To this end, I have experience in renewable energy (UK-China Cooperation on Climate Change), artificial intelligence (Harvard Berkman Klein Center), and sustainable growth (Cambridge Development Initiative). For more details, you can read my CV or connect with me on LinkedIn.

I care deeply about "doing good better" and am part of the effective altruism community. In my spare time, I co-host a podcast/blog called Hear This Idea and enjoy reading. Sometimes I also tweet my thoughts online.

Hear This Idea (Podcast/Blog)

I co-host a podcast "showcasing new thinking in philosophy and social sciences", together with my friend Fin Moorhouse. Each interview is accompanied by a in-depth write-up, which explores our guests' work in more detail. You can listen to Hear This Idea on all major podcast platforms or visit our website.
