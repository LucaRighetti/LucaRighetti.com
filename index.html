About Me

I am a Research Fellow at Open Philanthropy as part of the cause priortization team. On the side, I also co-host the Hear This Idea podcast, where Fin Moorhouse and I interview guests on "new thinking in philosophy, the social sciences, and effective altruism".

Previously, I was a research scholar at Oxford's Future of Humanity Institute, a research assistant at Oxford's Smith School of Enterprise and the Environment, and a policy advisor at the UK's Office for Articial Intelligence. Before that, I did a bachelors in economics at the University of Cambridge (where I made these revision notesrevision notes).

For more, check out my CV (and CV of Failures).
