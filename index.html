About Me

On the side, I also co-host a podcast called Hear This Idea, where I interview guests about ideas for solving important problems.

Previously, I was

  • a Research Scholar at University of Oxford's Future of Humanity Institute
  • a Policy Advisor to the UK Office for AI's work on the National AI Strategy
  • a Research Assistant at the Smith School's work on climate change risk.

Before all that, I did a Bachelors in Economics at the University of Cambridge (making revision notes). For more, check out my CV (and CV of Failures).
